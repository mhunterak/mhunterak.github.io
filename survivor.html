After the apocalypse, a lone survivor wanders the ruins, killing monsters and finding food.
How long can they survive? Will they be killed by a monster, or starve to death? Only one way to find out...

You can use your arrow keys to control the survivor, or set the threshold for their motivation.
This slider is essentially how hungry they need to be to switch from hunting to foraging.
if the HP dips below this level, they'll look for food to heal them.
