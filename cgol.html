Maxwell's Conway's Game of Life


Conway's Game of Life is a mathematical formua played out on a two dimensional grid.
Each cell is either alive or dead, and is born, survives, or dies based on its neighbors.

• if a cell is alive, and has less than two living neighbors, it dies from starvation.

• if a cell is alive, and has two or three living neighbors, it survives.

• if a cell is dead, and has three living neighbors, it is 'born', or becomes alive.

• if a cell is alive, and has more than three living neighbors, it dies from overpopulation.
